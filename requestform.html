Request for Issuing Certificates

Successful bidders from Col-Rice Auctions, or people to whom they have transferred their quota rights, may request certificates to be issued via the form below, or by emailing the Col-Rice administrator. If you do not receive an email confirmation of your request, please email us to make sure that we have received it. Certificates will be issued within two business days of receiving a request.

  • 1 MT brown rice = 0.89 MT milled rice equivalent
  • 1 MT rough (paddy) rice = 0.71 MT milled rice equivalent
